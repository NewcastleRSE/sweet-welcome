Welcome to the HT&Me Video

This video is part of the HT&Me support package. It has been developed by the SWEET research team, with input from doctors, breast care nurses and women with breast cancer. The aim of the video is to provide an introduction to hormone therapy, and why it's important to take it every day. It includes tips for setting a daily routine and what you can do about any side-effects you might have.

You can come back and watch this video as many times as you like. You may also want to share it with your friends and family if they are interested in learning more about what hormone therapy is.

You will also find this video on the main HT&Me website, which will be shared with you following your first consultation.
